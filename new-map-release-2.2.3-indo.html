Indonesia VMS Layer Added

Indonesia partnered with Global Fishing Watch to become the first nation to publicly release its Vessel Monitoring System data (VMS). This layer was first made available in a dedicated public workspace in June 2017, and we are now releasing it as a primary layer in our free, public map of global fishing activity. You will see a new Activity Layer “NAME OF LAYER”. The layer is turned on by default and the Indonesian VMS data is displayed on the map in GREEN. Learn more about about the Indonesian VMS and how our VMS initiative is changing the global conversation around transparency in commercial fishing.

First time user?

The map can be used for many purposes - to track individual vessels, monitor activity in marine protected areas, upload custom data onto the fishing activity layer to further your own research, and much more. Click and explore the map or check out our video tutorials to learn more.
