Welcome to Global Fishing Watch BETA Release 2.0 !

You can use this map to track commercial fishing around the world. For this new release, we’ve added some more helpful features.

WHAT’S NEW IN BETA 2.0

Now you can:

  • Upload your own custom data onto our fishing activity layer.
  • Access reports of fishing activity data within a selected MPA, RFMO or EEZ.
  • Share your workspace with anyone without registration.

Check out our tutorial video to learn more.

[For current users, all of your previously created workspaces are saved in the original map version and are still available through your workspace link.]
